Endpoints:

  • GET /time

  • GET /songs/by/:artist_name (search for all songs by a given artist)

  • GET /songs/name/:song_name (search for all songs with a given title)

  • GET /songs/:song_name/by/:artist_name (search for songs by artist AND title)

  • GET /songs/id/:song_id (find a song with a given ID)

  • GET /songs/buy (render buy menu)

  • POST /songs/buy/:song_id (buy a physical copy of song)

  • DELETE /songs/delete/:song_id (delete a song with a given ID)
