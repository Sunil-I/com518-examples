Endpoints:

  • GET /time

  • GET /products/by/:artist_name (search for all products by a given artist)

  • GET /products/name/:song_name (search for all products with a given title)

  • GET /products/:song_name/by/:artist_name (search for products by artist AND title)

  • GET /products/id/:song_id (find a song with a given ID)

  • GET /products/buy (render buy menu)

  • POST /products/buy/:song_id (buy a physical copy of song)

  • DELETE /products/delete/:song_id (delete a song with a given ID)
